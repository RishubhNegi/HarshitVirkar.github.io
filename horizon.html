HORIZON - 3 DAYS OF MADNESS

Horizon is the annual cultural festival of RAIT that is hosted by the Student Union Council of RAIT. For 26 years now, Horizon has been an integral part of each and every RAITian It's a 3-day festival with a lot of exciting and different day events happening on all the three day followed by the night events. On the first day, the first night event is the fierce dance competition followed by the glamorous fashion show. The other night events are the upbeat Mainstage Music Festival featuring international artists and the Bollywood Live-In musical night. Horizon is an experience no student would want to miss out because it's not only a festival, it's an emotion.

MMF - MAINSTAGE MUSIC FESTIVAL

MAINSTAGE MUSIC FESTIVAL (EDM Night), the BEST night event of the entire college year without any doubt. It was started 5 years back. MMF has gained fame over the years by getting artists like Sandro Silva, StadiumX, Marnik, Carl Nunes and many more. Unlike other college EDM nights in the country, we surely stand out by getting the best possible International line-ups. This gets the dance music fans from all over Mumbai & Navi-Mumbai together. The production is kept top-notch so that you go back with everlasting memories. MMF is nothing less than the large scale EDM concerts happening throughout the country, so if this is not your first experience, it will surely be one of your best concert experiences. The line-up gets better every year, so expect the unexpected for the 26th year of HORIZON.

LIVE IN

Like Bollywood? Love Bollywood? practice all your moves and get ready to dance off to upbeat indian tracks because horizon live in night is just the event for you.It is more than a decade old and it has transitioned over the years from hosting classic bands like JAL, Euphoria , parikrama to having over country's biggest musicians like Shankar Mahadevan, Bennny dayal, Suraj Jagan,Mohit Chauhan to name a few. The event has only grown since it's inception and it will only get bigger and better this year! We'll be waiting, see you soon.

FOOTLOOSE

After participating in a plethora of day events and having the sumptuous food crawl sort of experience at all the stall, it's time to witness the first night event of Horizon! Footloose is the dance competition hosted on the day 1 of the biggest cultural festival in Navi Mumbai which is fiercely competitive. The best teams in and around Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Thane participate with their quirky and innovative choreography. All the spectators are entertained by perfect set pieces performed by these teams. The sheer energy and enthusiasm of the raging crowd in the auditorium makes this event an experience in itself!

FASHION SHOW

The rich legacy that has been upheld by Horizon has encompassed Fashion Show for more than two decades. RAIT Fashion Show is not only a platform for students to compete but also to let loose and explore artistry. Contestants all over the city are thrilled to be a part of our high-toned contest. The themes chosen have proven to be innovative, expressive as well as awareness-oriented. Some of the most recognised personalities from the fashion industry have judged our event. We take great pride in stating that our college hosts one of the most prestigious and reputed Fashion Shows Competition in Mumbai.

DAY EVENTS

BOX CRICKET

Box cricket at best can be defined as the common man's tryst with the 'willow' and the 'leather'. A perfect early morning weekend getaway for the gymkhana hopping rich and the slum dwelling poor alike.What could be a better platform for the corporate slave who craves for weekends and the luggage carrying frustrated student who craves for his dull school going mornings to turn into bright ones.

CIPHER ARENA

Cypher Arena is a one on one B-Boying championship with smoke and earn format. Cypher Arena Volume-I had entries from places which are half way across the globe like Russia along with the entries from Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. The winner of Cypher Arena-I had also won the international B Boying competition organized by Red Bull called Red Bull BC One.

FOODATHON

"Food is not a word, it's a religion" said every food lover ever! Featured on the Campus Diaries by MTV, Foodathon is a fun event that sets your taste buds on fire. All the foodies around the campus participate in this mind-boggling event in hordes to have one-of-a-kind food knockout experience. The last one to survive the challenge, wins it against the horde.

AQUAZORB

Horizon, the cultural college festival of RAIT, is best known for it's captivating and adventurous day events. One of the most eye-catching events at Horizon is Aqua Zorb. Here, one can experience the absolute thrill of floating on water in a gigantic, transparent ball of air. Caged and placed in a spin cycle with dial cranked up to crazy. Safety is guaranteed by trained professionals right by your side.

WAR OF DJ's

It happened for the first time last year but it was a very successful day event and there's no explaining why. It was judged by one of the most famous bollywood DJ, Akhil Talreja. With more than 20 entries for the preliminary rounds, the audience experienced the best talents in the town. With so many budding DJ's around, War of DJ's is the perfect platform to show your skills.

MECHANICAL BULL

Mechanical bull, also known as a rodeo bull is a machine that replicates the sensation of riding a bucking animal, such as a rodeo bull. It was one of the most fun-filled and commercial day event. Almost everyone tried it once for the most obvious reason. Mechanical Bull is one of the traditional day event which happens every year and keeps the crowd involved.

RINK FOOTBALL ARENA

If playing football with your mates in a space with not the usual dimensions is what excites you,then bring your homies to the Rink Football Arena this horizon. Beat the best and win the Cup.

THE KING AND QUEEN OF HORIZON

Talented?competitive? Like being in the limelight? If the answer to all those is a YES,then this is just the place for you.Take part in RAIT's very own talent hunt sorts of event The KING and QUEEN of HORIZON. But only if you have it in you to be the KING or QUEEN

BUNGEE JUMPING

One of the unique experiences that Horizon gets to you is the Bungee Jumping. It is the only cultural fest in and around Navi Mumbai to leave you mesmerized with the extreme sport. Packed with excitement, students get all lined up to take the leap of faith in nowhere other than our campus. It is an experience that will leave you with the adrenaline rush that most people crave for!
